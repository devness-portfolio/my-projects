Privacy Policy

This policy explains how this public website handles contact information, technical data, third-party links, and patient communication boundaries.

Important Notice

Clinical and emergency boundaries

This website provides general information about Dr. Sadaf Ijaz MD's psychiatry practice. It is not a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency care.

Please do not send urgent concerns, emergencies, or highly sensitive medical details through regular email, social media, or external profile links. If this is an emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department.

Clinical records, patient rights, and treatment-related privacy practices may be governed by separate intake materials, appointment platform terms, and applicable health privacy laws. This website policy does not replace any Notice of Privacy Practices provided during care.

Information Collected

Information you choose to share

This static website does not include a contact form. If you call, email, or use an external booking or profile link, the information handled may include your name, contact details, appointment request details, message content, and any other information you choose to provide.

The website may also process standard technical information needed to load, secure, and maintain the site, such as browser type, device information, referring page, IP address, pages visited, and timestamps. This can happen through normal hosting, security, or server log activity.

How Information Is Used

Website and communication purposes

  • Responding to calls, emails, and appointment questions.
  • Helping patients reach booking, profile, or contact options.
  • Operating, maintaining, securing, and improving the website.
  • Complying with applicable legal, regulatory, or safety obligations.
Third Parties

External links and services

This website links to third-party services, including Headway, Psychology Today, LinkedIn, social media profiles, email, phone carriers, and Google-hosted fonts. These services are not controlled by this website and may have their own privacy notices, terms, cookies, analytics, or account settings.

Please review the privacy practices of any third-party service before sharing personal or health information there.

Sharing And Security

Limited sharing and reasonable safeguards

Personal information is not sold by this website. Information may be shared with service providers, booking platforms, communication providers, or other parties when needed to respond to an inquiry, support care-related administration, protect the website, comply with law, or with authorization.

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used for the website, but no internet transmission, email message, or online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

How to contact the practice

You may choose what information to share when calling or emailing the office. For appointment scheduling, insurance, or intake questions, use the available Headway booking link or contact the office directly.

To ask a privacy-related question about this website, contact: ssijaz@gmail.com or 804-682-7319.

Updates

Policy changes

This policy may be updated as the website, contact options, hosting, or practice operations change. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Quick Summary

Before you share information

  • This site does not use a patient contact form.
  • External booking and profile links have separate policies.
  • Regular email and social media are not emergency channels.
  • For emergencies, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department.
