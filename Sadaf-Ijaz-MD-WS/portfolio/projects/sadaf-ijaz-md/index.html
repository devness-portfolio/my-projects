Meet Your Physician

A Safe Space for Healing

I’m a psychiatrist helping adolescents, adults, and older adults navigate life’s mental health challenges with compassion and evidence based care. I specialize in treating anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, PTSD, panic disorders, and other mood related conditions.

My approach combines thoughtful medication management with supportive therapy and collaborative care tailored to each individual’s needs, goals, and lived experiences. I believe mental health care should feel safe, supportive, and personalized — not rushed or transactional. I take time to listen carefully, understand the full context of what you’re experiencing, and work with you to develop a treatment plan that feels realistic and sustainable.

I provide virtual psychiatric care in English, Urdu, and Punjabi to help make care more accessible and flexible for patients from diverse backgrounds.

  • Women’s mental health and reproductive psychiatry focus
  • Medication management with supportive therapy
  • Virtual visits for adolescents, adults, and older adults
  • Support for anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, PTSD, and PMDD-related concerns
