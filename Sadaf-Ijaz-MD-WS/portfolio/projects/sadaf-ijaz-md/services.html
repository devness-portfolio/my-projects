What to Expect

Steady care from first visit to follow-up.

I use appointments to understand what is happening, explain options in plain language, and build a plan that can be revisited as symptoms, stressors, and goals change.

  • Telepsychiatry access Virtual care is available for patients located in Virginia or Pennsylvania at the time of the visit.
  • Collaborative decisions Treatment planning may include medication, supportive psychotherapy, prevention strategies, or referrals when a different level of care is needed.
  • Clear communication Visits are designed to make next steps understandable, from evaluation through ongoing medication follow-up.
  1. 01

    Initial Evaluation

    We review symptoms, history, medications, prior care, and what you want treatment to help with now.

  2. 02

    Treatment Plan

    I explain options and help shape a plan that fits your needs, preferences, and clinical picture.

  3. 03

    Follow-Up Care

    Ongoing visits track progress, medication response, side effects, concerns, and changes in daily life.
